Welcome to my website.
It creates a random timetable for workers working throughout the week in 24-hour shifts. The Schedule takes the Array of workers and assigns them random days based on the roles they can perform (ex. leader, nightshift), so that all positions are filled. If a position in not filed it is displayed at the bottom of the day with the name of the role that is missing. Employee's hours are calculated as to not exceed 56 hours weekly and 176 hours monthly. If an employee is over the number of hours per month he/she is displayed with a different color.
